SHAPING TOMORROW'S FOOTBALL

Our ambitious roadmap for 2024-2030. Building Africa's premier football development institution.

Our 2030 Vision

Building on our decade of success, Magnet Football Academy is embarking on an ambitious journey to become Africa's undisputed leader in youth football development by 2030.

We envision a future where Tanzanian talent dominates African football and competes at the highest international levels. Our focus extends beyond developing players - we're building a sustainable ecosystem that includes professional teams, world-class facilities, and comprehensive education programs.

With support from strategic investors, including our Canadian partner, and growing corporate sponsorship, we're positioned for exponential growth while maintaining our core mission of equal opportunity for all.

Partner With Us

Strategic Focus Areas

Infrastructure Development: Building state-of-the-art training facilities across Tanzania with FIFA-standard pitches, gyms, and accommodation.

Talent Pipeline: Expanding our reach to identify and develop talent from every region of Tanzania.

International Partnerships: Strengthening collaborations with European clubs for player exchanges and coaching development.

Sustainability: Creating revenue streams through events, sponsorships, and player development fees to ensure long-term viability.

STRATEGIC ROADMAP 2024-2030

2024-2025

Foundation Strengthening

Maintain solid structure for youth and senior teams. Focus on younger cohorts (born 2015+). Reduce ticket costs for greater accessibility.

2025-2026

Competitive Excellence

Keep current squad competitive while evolving talent. Win Gothia Cup championship. Expand international tournament participation.

2026-2028

Infrastructure Development

Build Magnet Football Complex with multiple pitches, gym, medical center, and dormitories. Establish regional high-performance centers.

2028-2030

African Leadership

Become Africa's leading football academy. Develop first Tanzanian player in top European league. Establish professional women's program.
