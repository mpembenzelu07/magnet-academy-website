FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What ages do you accept?

We train boys and girls from 4 to 19 years old, with programs tailored to each age group's developmental needs.

When does training take place?

Training sessions are scheduled after school hours and on weekends. Specific schedules vary by age group and program.

What are the fees?

As a non-profit organization, we offer affordable fees with scholarship opportunities for talented players in need.

Do you provide equipment?

Basic training equipment is provided. Players need appropriate sports attire and football boots.
