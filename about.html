Our Story & Leadership

Founded in 2014 with a vision for youth development

Who We Are

Magnet Youth Sports Academy (MYSA), also known as Magnet Youth Soccer Organization (MYSO), is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing equal opportunities for children and youth through sports in Tanzania.

We train boys and girls from 4 to 19 years old, focusing not just on football skills but on developing well-rounded individuals through discipline, leadership, and teamwork.
