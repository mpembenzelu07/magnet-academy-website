OUR STORY OF IMPACT

From humble beginnings in 2014 to Tanzania's premier youth sports academy. Building champions, shaping character, and creating opportunities.

Our Mission & Vision

Founded in 2014, Magnet Youth Sports Academy (MYSA), also known as Magnet Youth Soccer Organization (MYSO), began with a simple vision: to provide equal opportunities for Tanzanian youth through sports.

What started as a small community initiative has grown into a nationally recognized non-profit organization that trains boys and girls from 4 to 19 years old, focusing not just on athletic excellence but on holistic personal development.

Our Core Mission

To develop well-rounded individuals through sports, emphasizing discipline, leadership, teamwork, and dedication while providing pathways to professional football and international competition.

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading youth sports academy, recognized for producing not just exceptional athletes, but responsible citizens and future leaders.
