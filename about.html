Our Story & Vision

About Magnet Youth Sports Academy

Founded in 2014, Magnet Youth Sports Academy (MYSA), also known as Magnet Youth Soccer Organization (MYSO), is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing equal opportunities for children and youth through sports.

Our Mission

To develop well-rounded individuals through sports, emphasizing discipline, leadership, teamwork, and dedication while providing pathways to professional football and international competition.
