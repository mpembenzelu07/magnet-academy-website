OUR STORY OF TRANSFORMATION

From 12 centers to 300+, from local fields to international glory. Building Tanzania's football future since 2014.

Leading Youth Development Through Football

Founded in 2014, Magnet Football Academy began with a revolutionary vision: to provide equal opportunity to all children and youth in Tanzania through sports. What started as 12 centers with a handful of passionate coaches has grown into Tanzania's largest and most successful youth football development institution.

Our mission extends beyond creating skilled footballers. We focus on holistic personal and professional growth, building character, discipline, and leadership qualities that last a lifetime. We believe every child deserves the chance to discover their potential in a supportive, fun-filled environment.

Our Core Mission

To build confidence and love for the game while creating a dynamic learning environment that motivates children and helps them reach their full potential both on and off the field.

Our Vision

To be recognized as Africa's leading youth football program, developing not just exceptional athletes but responsible citizens and future leaders who represent Tanzania with pride on global stages.

OUR COACHING PHILOSOPHY

Passion First

We nurture intrinsic love for the game. Practice must be fun and entertaining, building confidence and enjoyment that lasts a lifetime.

Individual Development

We recognize skills develop at different rates. Our coaches look beyond technical abilities to understand each player's unique journey and potential.

Team Chemistry

Building friendship and strong team bonds is fundamental. Team chemistry transforms talented individuals into championship teams.

Winning With Values

While we compete to win, victory never comes at the expense of our core values. We pursue excellence with integrity and sportsmanship.
