ELITE TRAINING PROGRAMS

Structured development pathways for ages 4-19. Building technical excellence, tactical intelligence, and championship character.

AGE GROUP PROGRAMS

AGES 4-6

FOUNDATION STAGE

Introduction to football through fun games and basic motor skills development. Building love for the game.

  • Basic ball control & coordination
  • Fundamental motor skills
  • Introduction to team play
  • Fun-based learning environment
  • 2 training sessions per week
AGES 7-12

DEVELOPMENT STAGE

Technical skill mastery and introduction to tactical understanding. Building football fundamentals.

  • Technical skill development
  • Basic tactical understanding
  • Small-sided games
  • Physical literacy & agility
  • 3 training sessions + weekend games
AGES 13-19

PERFORMANCE STAGE

Advanced training for competitive football. Focus on tactical awareness and mental preparation.

  • Advanced tactical training
  • Position-specific development
  • Physical conditioning
  • Leadership development
  • 4-5 sessions + competitive matches

PROFESSIONAL PATHWAY

From grassroots to professional football - A clear development journey

STEP 1

YOUTH ACADEMY

Age 4-12: Foundational training in our 300+ centers nationwide. Focus on technical skills and love for the game.

STEP 2

ELITE ACADEMY TEAMS

Age 13-16: Selection into elite squads. Advanced tactical training and regional competitions.

STEP 3

INTERNATIONAL EXPOSURE

Age 16-19: Participation in Gothia Cup (Sweden), Iber Cup (Portugal), and other international tournaments.

STEP 4

PROFESSIONAL PATHWAY

Path to Tanzania Premier League through our First League team. National team selections and overseas opportunities.
