OUR TRAINING PROGRAMS

Structured development pathways for youth aged 4-19. Building skills, character, and champions.

AGE GROUP PROGRAMS

FOUNDATION STAGE

Ages 4-6 Years

Introduction to football through fun games and basic motor skills development. Focus on enjoyment and love for the sport.

  • Basic ball control
  • Motor skills development
  • Team play introduction
  • Fun-based learning

DEVELOPMENT STAGE

Ages 7-12 Years

Technical skill development and introduction to tactical understanding. Building football fundamentals and teamwork.

  • Technical skill development
  • Basic tactical understanding
  • Small-sided games
  • Physical literacy

PERFORMANCE STAGE

Ages 13-19 Years

Advanced training for competitive football. Focus on tactical awareness, physical conditioning, and mental preparation.

  • Advanced tactical training
  • Physical conditioning
  • Competitive match play
  • Leadership development

SPECIALIZED TRAINING PROGRAMS

TECHNICAL SKILLS PROGRAM

Master the fundamentals of football with focused technical training:

  • Ball control and dribbling mastery
  • Passing accuracy and vision
  • Shooting technique and power
  • First touch development
  • Position-specific skills

TACTICAL INTELLIGENCE PROGRAM

Develop game understanding and decision-making skills:

  • Positional awareness training
  • Game strategy and systems
  • Decision-making under pressure
  • Set-piece specialization
  • Match analysis sessions

PHYSICAL DEVELOPMENT PROGRAM

Build athleticism and prevent injuries with scientific training:

  • Age-appropriate strength training
  • Speed and agility development
  • Endurance and stamina building
  • Flexibility and mobility work
  • Injury prevention programs

CHARACTER DEVELOPMENT PROGRAM

Build leadership and life skills beyond the football field:

  • Leadership and teamwork workshops
  • Discipline and time management
  • Academic support and tutoring
  • Community service projects
  • Career guidance sessions
